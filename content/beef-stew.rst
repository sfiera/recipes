Beef Stew
=========

:category: Mains

Ingredients
-----------

.. include:: scale.rsti

* **1tbsp** butter
* **1tbsp** olive oil
* **650g** beef, cubed
* **1** onion, chopped
* **2 cloves** garlic, minced
* **2tbsp** flour
* **1tsp** salt
* **1tsp** pepper
* **3** carrots, sliced
* **5** small potatoes, cubed
* **1tbsp** Dijon mustard
* **240mL** dry red wine
* **240mL** beef broth
* **120mL** red wine vinegar
* **1tsp** dried thyme
* **2tbsp** dark brown sugar
* **200g** green beans, trimmed and halved

Instructions
------------

1.  Heat butter and oil in pot
2.  Brown beef in pot and remove
3.  Saut√© onion and garlic in pot and remove
4.  Mix flour, salt, and pepper
5.  Toss beef, onion, and garlic in flour mixture
6.  Add everything except green beans to pot
7.  Bring pot to boil over medium heat
8.  Boil stew for 90 minutes
9.  Add green beans to pot
10. Boil another 5 minutes
