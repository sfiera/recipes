Egg Nog
=======

Ingredients
-----------

* **4** egg yolks
* **50g** sugar
* **400mL** milk
* **200mL** heavy cream
* **1 tsp** nutmeg
* **4** egg whites

Instructions
------------

1.  Beat egg yolks until lightened in color.
2.  Mix sugar into egg yolks.
3.  Add milk, cream, and nutmeg.
4.  Beat egg whites to stiff peaks.
5.  Fold egg whites into mixture.
