Egg Nog
=======

:category: Drinks

Ingredients
-----------

.. include:: scale.rsti

* **4** eggs
* **50g** sugar
* **400mL** milk
* **200mL** heavy cream
* **1 tsp** nutmeg

Instructions
------------

1.  Separate eggs.
2.  Beat egg yolks until lightened in color.
3.  Mix sugar into egg yolks.
4.  Add milk, cream, and nutmeg.
5.  Beat egg whites to stiff peaks.
6.  Fold egg whites into mixture.
