Pecan Pie
=========

:category: Sweets

Ingredients
-----------

* **2** eggs
* **180g** brown sugar
* **50g** sugar
* **100g** butter
* **8g** flour
* **15g** milk
* **1 tsp** vanilla
* **150g** pecans, chopped
* pie shell

Instructions
------------

1.  Beat eggs until foamy.
2.  Melt butter and add to eggs.
3.  Mix in sugar and flour.
4.  Mix in milk, vanilla, and nuts.
5.  Pour mixture into pie shell.
6.  Bake at 200°C for 10 minutes.
7.  Bake at 180°C for 35 minutes.
